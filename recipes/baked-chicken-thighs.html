Cuban Baked Chicken

Description

Cuban style baked chicken thighs.

Ingredients

  • 2 bone-in chicken breasts cut in half
  • 2 bone-in chicken thighs
  • 2 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • cooking spray
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix onion, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, allspice, onion powder, cumin, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  2. Add chicken pieces, stir. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight
  3. Preheat oven to 375F. Lightly grease a baking dish with cooking spray.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet. Pan-fry chicken until browned (about 5 minutes per side).
  5. Transfer chicken to prepared baking dish. Pour remaining marinade on top.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes at 375F.
